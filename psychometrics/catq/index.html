The CAT-Q

Please read each statement below and choose the answer that best fits your experiences during social interactions. Please note that you have to score this test yourself; the radio buttons are included so you can keep track of your answers. Statements with an asterisk (*) should be reverse-scored, which we will explain in the Results section after the test.
