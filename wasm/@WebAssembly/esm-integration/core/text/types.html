Result Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef3089#1{{}}\mathdef3089{result type} & \href{../text/types.html#text-resulttype}{\mathtt{resulttype}} &::=& (t{:}\href{../text/types.html#text-functype}{\mathtt{result}})^? &\Rightarrow& [t^?] \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Abbreviations¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef3089#1{{}}\mathdef3089{parameter} & \def\mathdef3159#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3159{(}~~\def\mathdef3160#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3160{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3161#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3161{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3162#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3162{(}~~\def\mathdef3163#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3163{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3164#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3164{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef3089#1{{}}\mathdef3089{result} & \def\mathdef3165#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3165{(}~~\def\mathdef3166#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3166{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3167#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3167{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3168#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3168{(}~~\def\mathdef3169#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3169{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3170#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3170{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
